# Bridges-26---Team-TokTok# Emergency Info Holder

## Problem Definition
In an emergancy situation where;
- patient may be unconscious
- No time to search systems
- No access to full medical history
  
This systems allows people in emergancy situations to access important medical 
information for other patient who may not be able to let them know in that situation.

## Traditional solutions:
- Contact family members or emergancy contacts to get information about the patient.
- Having the information on paper on the patient at all time.
